Karaca Arboretum

Established in 1980 by Hayrettin Karaca, also known as "Grandpa Earth," this site is a living tree museum that hosts thousands of plant species from all over the world. It is the first private arboretum in Turkey and serves as a critical gene reserve for endangered plant species.

Arboretum Facts
Established 1980
Founder Hayrettin Karaca (Founder of TEMA)
Total Area 13.5 Hectares
Number of Species Over 7,000 different plant species
Location Samanlı Village, Yalova
