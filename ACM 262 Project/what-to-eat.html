Traditional Tastes of Yalova

Discover the unique flavors that define Yalova's culinary culture.

Top Delicacies

Yalova Sütlüsü

A light, milky dessert flavored with coconut and walnuts. It is the signature sweet of the city.

Termal Sarma

A delicious main course made with steak, special sauce, and local vegetables.

Yalova Kiwi

Yalova is the largest kiwi producer in Turkey. The local kiwi is known for its intense aroma.
