Sudüşen Waterfall

Sudüşen Waterfall (Sudüşen Şelalesi) is one of the most breathtaking natural wonders located near the village of Üvezpınar. Surrounded by a dense forest, the waterfall offers a cool and refreshing atmosphere, making it a favorite spot for visitors seeking an escape into nature.

Key Features

  • Spectacular Views: A perfect backdrop for photography and nature observation.
  • Natural Cooling: The mist from the waterfall provides a natural air-conditioning effect in summer.
  • Hiking and Picnic Areas: The surrounding trails are ideal for trekking lovers and families.
